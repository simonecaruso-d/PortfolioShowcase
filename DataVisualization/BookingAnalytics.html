01 · Problem

No visibility into how the workplace was actually used

Reservations existed in the system but no one could tell whether booked spaces were actually occupied, whether capacity was being hit, or whether physical access matched digital bookings

Before

  • Booking data locked in the PostgreSQL backend: no aggregated view of utilization or saturation across the coworking space
  • No way to compare physical entry/exit events from time-keeper hardware against digital reservation data: access anomalies invisible
  • Capacity planning based on headcount alone, without hour-by-hour occupancy snapshots or frequency distribution analysis
  • Multi-tenant guest data handled without a formal GDPR-compliant analytics layer: PGP-encrypted fields not surfaced safely

After

  • Live connection to two Azure PostgreSQL databases via native SQL folding: reservation and access data available to eFM and clients as well
  • Physical entry/exit events reconciled against digital check-in/out: access anomalies flagged and filterable by tenant, date, and resource
  • Hour-by-hour saturation snapshots on a 30-minute calendar: frequency bands show under / medium / over capacity distribution
  • PGP-encrypted guest email columns handled safely: data segregation and dynamic filtering upon access, based on permissions
02 · Solution

Four analytics surfaces, one platform

Each surface answers a different operational question: how much of the space is used, when it peaks, who is using it, and whether physical presence matches digital intent

UTL

PPL

ACC

Physical entry/exit device events vs. digital reservation check-in/out. Mismatches flagged per tenant for security and compliance monitoring

04 · DAX

The logic, in code

45+ measures across utilization, saturation, occupancy, people, and guest access. Standout complexity: measures with multi-denominator utilization math and the Offered Time Independent pattern that strips date filter context while preserving all other filters

Occupancy measure
People currently inside the space at the selected hour: count of reservations with an active status whose hour range matches the Calendar slicer. Drives the real-time occupancy KPI card on the Today Monitoring page
DAX
 1VAR SelectedHour = SELECTEDVALUE('Calendar'[Hour], BLANK())
 2RETURN CALCULATE(COUNTROWS('Reservation Flat'),
 3    'Reservation Flat'[Reservation Status] IN {"Booked", "Closed"},
 4    'Reservation Flat'[Hour Range] = SelectedHour)
Utilization Booked measure
Booked resource-hours as a fraction of total offered resource-hours. Numerator excludes cancelled and no-show rows. Paired with Utilization Offered to surface the gap between offer and confirmed demand
DAX
 1RETURN DIVIDE(CALCULATE(SUM('Reservation Flat'[Duration [h]]),
 2              'Reservation Flat'[Reservation Status] IN {"Booked", "Closed"}),
 3              [Offered Time Independent] * [Hours Per Day])
People Covering measure
Unique active employees who made at least one non-cancelled reservation in the selected period, divided by total active headcount from Get Users. Workspace adoption rate KPI: used in the Recurring variant to filter bookers with 12+ reservations in the past 12 months
DAX
 1RETURN DIVIDE(CALCULATE(DISTINCTCOUNT('Reservation Flat'[Created By]),'Reservation Flat'[Reservation Status] <> "Cancelled"),
 2              COUNTROWS('Get Users'))
Exits measure
Count of physical exit events recorded by access-service hardware in the selected period. Cross-referenced against digital check-out records on the Access Anomalies page to flag discrepancies where exits exceed or precede reservation check-outs
DAX
 1RETURN CALCULATE(COUNTROWS('Access'), 'Access'[Event Type] = "Exit")
Is User Recurrent? column · Reservation Flat
Classifies a booker as "recurrent" if they have made at least 12 non-cancelled reservations in the past 12 months. Used to filter the Recurring Bookers and Recurring People Covering measures, removing infrequent users from adoption rate calculations to avoid inflating the metric
DAX
 1VAR Email          = 'Reservation Flat'[Created By]
 2VAR RecentBookings = CALCULATE(COUNTROWS('Reservation Flat'),
 3                               FILTER('Reservation Flat',
 4                                      'Reservation Flat'[Created By] = Email
 5                                      && 'Reservation Flat'[Check In Time] >= TODAY() - 365
 6                                      && 'Reservation Flat'[Reservation Status] <> "Cancelled"))
 7RETURN IF(RecentBookings >= 12, "Y", "N")
Reservation Start Time column · Reservation Flat
Calculate the actual start time of a reservation considering the timezone and status of the reservation
DAX
 1VAR Timezone       = RELATED ('Thing Flat'[Timezone])
 2VAR Intermediate   = IF ('Reservation Flat'[Reservation Status] IN ["Closed", "In Progress"],
 3                        ('Reservation Flat'[Check In Time], ('Reservation Flat'[Check In Time] + 1/24))
 4VAR Difference     = LOOKUPVALUE ('UTC Offset'[UTC offset ±hh:mm]), ('UTC Offset'[TZ database name]), Timezone)

 5RETURN  Intermediate + Difference/24
